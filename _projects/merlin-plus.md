**MERLIN+P+TFA** integrates promoter-derived priors and transcription factor activity estimation to improve gene regulatory network inference in bulk and single-cell data, with systematic benchmarking and experimental validation of predicted regulatory interactions.

- **Code:** <https://github.com/Roy-lab/MERLIN-P-TFA>
- **Preprint:** <https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.06.09.658650v2>
